G'day Mate

I'm Luke, a self-declared Aussie in mind and surfer at heart.

I love literature, technology, and planning my life on the Gold Coast of Australia in Paradise. If you like the beach, photography, or tinkering with Material Design then you've come to the right place.

When disconnected from the web, I volunteer in my community to share Bible truths with my neighbors, study the Bible daily, and practice skateboarding.
